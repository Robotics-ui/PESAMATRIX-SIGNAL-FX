About Trading Days
✓ Counted Days
Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday — these are trading days that count toward your subscription.
✗ Not Counted
Saturday and Sunday are weekends and do not count. The system automatically skips them when calculating expiry.
Example
Buy 5 days on a Friday → expires the following Friday (skips Sat & Sun automatically).
