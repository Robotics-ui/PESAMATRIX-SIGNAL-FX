FEATURE 3: SUBSCRIPTION PURCHASE SYSTEM

Create a subscription purchase page.

Requirements:

Display:

- Fee Per Trading Day.
- Minimum Trading Days.
- Maximum Trading Days.

Create a slider that allows users to select the number of subscription days.

IMPORTANT:

PMATRIX subscription days are TRADING DAYS ONLY.

Trading days are:

- Monday
- Tuesday
- Wednesday
- Thursday
- Friday

Exclude:

- Saturday
- Sunday

The system must calculate subscription expiry using trading days only.

Example:

If a user purchases 20 trading days on Friday:

The system skips Saturday and Sunday and continues counting from Monday.

Real-time calculation:

Total Amount = Selected Trading Days × Fee Per Trading Day

Display:

- Selected Trading Days
- Fee Per Trading Day
- Total Amount
- Estimated Expiry Date

Prepare for M-Pesa payment integration.
